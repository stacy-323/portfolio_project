Providing Design for Local Organizations

boons provides restaurants with a platform that streamlines online ordering, custom website branding, flexible delivery options, catering, and data-driven marketing tools.

Our mission is to help restaurants thrive online without compromising control or profitability. We enable restaurant owners to take control of their growth and help consumers directly support local businesses.

“

Goal

To design a seamless and user-friendly food ordering and management website and app to empower local businesses and enhance consumer satisfaction.

”

Rising operational costs are creating a shift in the restaurant industry, requiring owners to create a strong digital presence and a way to manage customer data, relationships, and delivery logistics. They turn to online marketplaces, but these marketplaces are not designed for independent, owner-operated restaurants. They are cut into profits, limit access to customer data, and provide little digital growth due to restricted branding.
